About Portfolio Style Career & Achievements





Dana Tanamachi, a Japanese-Mexican American, is a Texas-born graphic designer celebrated for her chalk lettering and hand-drawn typography. Her cultural heritage and upbringing have cultivated a deep respect for traditional craftsmanship, visible in her detailed and distinctive designs. Relocating to New York, she refined her vintage-inspired, contemporary style, eventually building a portfolio that spans branding, packaging, and large-scale murals. Tanamachi's work, with its artistic, personalized touch, connects with diverse audiences and brings a unique, handcrafted quality to each project.
